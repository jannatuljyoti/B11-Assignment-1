The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 envisions a future driven by technology, inclusive growth, and sustainability. Focused on renewable energy, digital transformation, robust infrastructure,
and innovation, it aspires to become a global leader, ensuring prosperity and equality for all citizens.
